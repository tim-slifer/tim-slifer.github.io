maturation within the QA automation space. My role consists of tool selection and development, establishing processes,

their level of expertise. I work primarily in a Java environment, and do have experience on the .NET side as well.

utilities for which I've found a need over time. Other projects include thought-exercises, proofs of concepts, and the 
occasional web scraper. I've also been spending time working with Spring Boot writing web services.
