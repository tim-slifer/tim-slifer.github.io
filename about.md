maturation within the QA automation space. My role consists of tool selection and development, establishing processes, 
aligning people and skill sets to roles within the team, and mentoring or providing training to those looking to advance
their level of expertise. I focus my efforts in a Java environment, and do have a bit of experience on the .NET side 
as well.

utilities for which I've found a need over time, and sometimes working on the occasional web-scraper. Other 
projects consist mainly of fun thought-exercises and proofs of concepts.
